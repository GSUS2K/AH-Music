Privacy Policy

AH Music respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we process and why.

1. Data We Collect

  • User IDs: To manage playback requests and active sessions.
  • Guild IDs: To maintain queues for specific Discord servers.
  • Voice States: To connect to and disconnect from audio channels only when requested.

2. Data Usage

We use your data strictly to provide the music streaming service. We do not use your data for profiling or advertising.

3. Data Retention

All playback data (queues, active tracks) is stored in volatile memory and is cleared automatically when the bot restarts or the queue is finished. We do not maintain long-term databases of your listening history.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the YouTube Data API and YouTube-DL to retrieve audio metadata. Your interaction with these services through the bot is subject to their respective privacy policies.
